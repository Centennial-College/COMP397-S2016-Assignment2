Instructions

Including you, there are two people on the stranded island. You are given 10 hours to head to the center of the island. At the center you will enter a portal and be able to safely escape. Only one person is permitted to use the portal to escape. After 10 hours have passed, nuclear missles will be launched targetting the island and anyone remaining on the island will be killed. Good luck escaping the island!
